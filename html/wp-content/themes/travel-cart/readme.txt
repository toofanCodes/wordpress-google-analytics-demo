== Description ==

Travel Cart is a lightweight and responsive WordPress theme designed for travel bloggers, tour operators, and travel agencies looking to create a stunning online presence. With its sleek design, SEO-friendly structure, and fast-loading performance, Travel Cart ensures an immersive experience for travelers exploring destinations, itineraries, and travel guides. The theme is compatible with popular plugins, allowing seamless integration of booking systems, maps, and galleries to showcase breathtaking travel experiences. Whether you're sharing travel tips, promoting vacation packages, or running a travel magazine, Travel Cart is the perfect solution for a captivating and user-friendly travel website.
