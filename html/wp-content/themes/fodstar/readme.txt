== Description ==
Fodstar is a robust and dynamic Elementor WordPress theme designed for food listings and multi-author platforms. Perfect for food enthusiasts, culinary experts, and food vendors, Fodstar allows you to create a vibrant online community where users can list, review, and explore various food-related businesses and products. Whether you're building a comprehensive food directory or a multi-author food site, fodstar offers all the tools you need to make your vision a reality.

== Features ==

* Responsive Design: Ensure your site looks perfect on any device, from desktops to smartphones.
Customizable Layouts: Choose from multiple pre-designed layouts or create your own using our intuitive drag-and-drop page builder.

* Multi-Author Support: Enable multiple authors to create and manage their own listings, fostering a community of diverse voices and perspectives.

* Food Listings: Feature detailed food and vendor listings, complete with descriptions, images, ratings, and reviews.

* Advanced Search and Filters: Allow users to easily search and filter listings by category, location, rating, and more.

* User Submissions: Allow users to submit their own food and vendor listings, enhancing the richness and variety of your site.

* Detailed Documentation: Comprehensive documentation and dedicated support to help you get the most out of fodstar.

Yes you can use any of the popular plugin.


= How to install fodstar theme=
1. Login your WordPress dashboard.
2. Go to "Appearance" -> "Theme" and click on "Add New" button.
3. Click on "Upload Theme" button and choose the theme's zip file you have downloaded.
4. Click on "Install Now" button.
5. Activate the theme.
6. Now go to "Appearance" -> "Customize" to configure theme options.

Alternatively,

1. Login to your WordPress dashboard.
2. Go to "Appearance" -> "Theme" and click on "Add New" button.
3. Search for "fodstar".
4. Click on Install and Activate.
